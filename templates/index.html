About ACOODA

Digital On-Duty Management

ACOODA replaces the traditional paper-based OD process with a fully digital workflow, ensuring faster approvals, better tracking, and complete transparency.

Students

Apply for OD online, track approval status in real-time, and download your OD letter as a PDF instantly.

Tutors

Review and approve student OD applications for your assigned class with remark capability.

HOD

Give final approval, generate department reports, and maintain complete oversight of OD applications.

Admin

Manage staff uploads via Excel, monitor all users, and maintain system-wide audit logs.
